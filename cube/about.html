ABOUT

Interior Designer Cube Studio’s work has been published in MSP Home&Design, StarTribune, Artful Living, Traditional Home, Midwest Home, CityPages, and PaperCity. It is included in 2018 Asid MN Design's “A List” of the best designers in USA.
Cube Studio is a Minneapolis based interior designer studio founded by sisters Jayne and Joan Michaels, whose style is at once practical and sophisticated marked by everyday luxury and elegant simplicity with a modern view. Since 2005, their work has included many ground-up construction projects, from a showcase for a blue-chip contemporary art collection in Wisconsin, to an Spaniard style summerhouse in upstate New York and a Shaker inspired farmhouse in Minnesota. In Twin Cities, their designs include two pieds-a-terre on St. Paul Downtown and a family apartment on Hennepin Ave. No matter the style or setting, Cube believes that a harmony of color, pattern, texture and scale is essential. Rather than impose a signature look, they attune to their clients’ needs and aesthetics.
